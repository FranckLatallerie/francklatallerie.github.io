SOLA SW 2D

Data related with Latallerie et al., 2022, GJI.

SOLASW2D_50 SOLASW2D_100 SOLASW2D_150 SOLASW2D_200 SOLASW2D_250 SOLASW2D_300 SOLASW2D_350 SOLASW2D_400

Input data are path-averaged velocity versus depth profiles. These data are from Maggi et al., 2006.

All tomography results: tomography Vs model, 2D resolution and uncertainties for every model parameter.

In future I will provide a clean suite of codes specifically adapted to these data to display them and filter other types of data to the tomographic resolution.
